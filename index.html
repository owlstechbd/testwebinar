About the whitepaper

As AI and cloud adoption rise, government and regulated sectors prioritize data sovereignty, driving the need for sovereign enabled public cloud solutions that give them the freedom to innovate without compromising security, compliance, or national data control.

This whitepaper delves into the importance of data sovereignty, the challenges of cloud adoption in regulated sectors, and how Core42 Sovereign Public Cloud delivers the best of both worlds: Microsoft Azure’s hyperscale capabilities combined with UAE-specific controls leveraging Core42’s Insight application for a secure, compliant, and innovation-ready cloud environment.

In this whitepaper, you will explore:

  • The role of sovereign enabled public cloud in UAE’s digital transformation.
  • The importance of data sovereignty in the public sector and regulated industries.
  • Two models of sovereignty in cloud: private vs. public.
  • Sovereign enabled public cloud for the public sector and regulated industries.
  • Best practices in migrating to sovereign enabled public cloud.
  • Overview of Core42 Sovereign Public Cloud.
  • Competitive advantages of Core42 Sovereign Public Cloud.
